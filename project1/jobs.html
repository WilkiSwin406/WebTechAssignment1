Cloud Engineer, Level 1

As one of our cloud engineers, you will be responsible for designing, implementing and maintaining our cloud-based infrastructure and services. You must ensure that our cloud systems are scalable, secure and reliable at all times. Your position will primarily consist of maintenance work as a level one employee.

  • Position Reference Number: CLE01
  • Salary range: $100,000 - $105,100
  • Supervisor position: Cloud Engineer, Level 3 (CLE03)
  • Essential requirements:
    1. 2+ years of experience in cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP)
    2. Strong understanding of networking, security, and cloud architecture
    3. Proficient in scripting languages (e.g., Python, Bash)
  • Preferable:
    1. Experience with containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
    2. Knowledge of cost optimization strategies in the cloud

Database Engineer, Level 1

As a database analyst, you will be responsible for creating efficient database systems, optimizing queries, and implementing backup and recovery strategies for our databases. Your position will also include handling scaling and integration tasks, as well as database migration if necessary. As a level one employee, you will primarily focus on maintenance.

  • Position Reference Number: DBE01
  • Salary range: $105,000 - $115,000
  • Supervisor position: Database Engineer, Level 3 (DBE03)
  • Essential requirements:
    1. 2+ years of experience in data analysis or related field
    2. Proficiency in data visualization tools (e.g., Power BI, Tableau, Looker)
    3. Understanding of statistical analysis and data interpretation
  • Preferable:
    1. Proficiency in Python or R for data analysis
    2. Experience with big data tools (e.g., Spark, Hadoop)

Data Analyst, Level 2

As a data analyst, you will be responsible for collecting, processing and analysing data in order to assist management to make informed decisions. You will identify trends, patterns and insights through data visualisation and statistical techniques. As a level two employee, you will also be expected to prepare reports for stakeholders and management, as well as participate in meetings in order to share your findings.

  • Position Reference Number: DTA02
  • Salary range: $110,000 - $135,000
  • Supervisor position: Data Analyst, Level 3 (DTA03)
  • Essential requirements:
    1. 4+ years of experience in database development and administration
    2. Proficiency in SQL and database design principles
    3. Experience with at least one RDBMS (e.g., PostgreSQL, MySQL, SQL Server)
  • Preferable:
    1. Experience with NoSQL databases (e.g., MongoDB, Cassandra)
    2. Certification in database technologies

UX Designer, Level 1

As one of our UX designers, you will be responsible for the user experience, including tasks such as upkeep of our website and app's user interface and designing prototypes or wireframes. You must ensure that your work is functional, readable and aesthetically pleasing. As a level one employee, you will primarily undertake smaller UI design tasks directly under your supervisor.

  • Position Reference Number: UXD01
  • Salary range: $90,000 - $105,000
  • Supervisor position: UX Designer, Level 3 (UXD03)
  • Essential requirements:
    1. 2+ years of experience in UX design
    2. Strong portfolio demonstrating UX processes and final designs
    3. Knowledge of responsive and accessible design principles
  • Preferable:
    1. Experience working in Agile or cross-functional teams
    2. Familiarity with HTML/CSS and design handoff to developers
